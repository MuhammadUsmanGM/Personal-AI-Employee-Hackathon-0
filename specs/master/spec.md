# Feature Specification: AI Employee Communication Channels

## Overview
Enable the AI employee to communicate through dedicated email, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp accounts for autonomous business operations.

## Functional Requirements
- AI employee can send/receive emails from its dedicated account
- AI employee can send/receive LinkedIn messages
- AI employee can send/receive WhatsApp messages
- All communication follows company brand guidelines
- Communication logging and audit trail maintained
- Human approval workflow for sensitive communications

## Non-functional Requirements
- Security: End-to-end encryption for stored credentials
- Availability: 99% uptime for communication channels
- Performance: Response time under 30 seconds
- Compliance: Adherence to platform terms of service

## Constraints
- Must comply with email, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp terms of service
- No spam or unsolicited messaging
- Proper rate limiting to avoid account suspension
- Secure credential storage (no plain text)
